Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mental Health Fund?

R2.94 from every entry (4%) goes to TrueSelf Wellness Circles - weekly confidential support groups in George for mental health, addiction recovery, and community healing.

Where does my money go?

R70 entry: R60.20 to competition costs, R6.86 to physical healthcare, R2.94 to mental health support. All funds stay in George, South Africa.

What are TrueSelf Wellness Circles?

Weekly confidential support groups providing safe spaces for mental health discussions, addiction recovery, and community healing in George.
