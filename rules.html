Complete Competition Rules

TrueSelf #NoFilterGiveBack - Detailed terms and conditions for our authentic selfie competition

1. Campaign Purpose

A skill-based competition where authentic selfies (submitted with a R70 entry fee) help fund mental health support and community wellness in George, South Africa.

14% (R9.80) of each entry goes directly to charity.

2. Eligibility

  • South African residents aged 18 and above
  • Valid South African ID or passport required
  • Employees of TrueSelf and immediate family members are not eligible

3. Entry Requirements

STRICTLY PROHIBITED

  • Filters, effects, or digital alterations
  • Professional photography or studio shots
  • Photos taken by others
  • Inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content
  • Copyrighted material or third-party content

REQUIRED

  • Original selfie taken by the entrant
  • Natural lighting and authentic appearance
  • Clear, high-quality image
  • Appropriate, respectful content

4. Entry Fee & Donation

R70 entry fee with 14% going to charity

Entry Fee Breakdown

  • Total Entry Fee: R70.00
  • Charity Donation: R9.80 (14%)
  • Platform & Processing: R60.20 (86%)

Charity Distribution

  • Mental Health: 100% of donation

All donations are processed securely through PayFast and distributed to verified charity partners in George, South Africa.

5. Prizes & Judging

Prizes will be announced as the competition grows and more entries are received. Stay tuned for exciting prize reveals!

Judging Criteria

  • Authenticity and natural beauty
  • Creativity and originality
  • Adherence to competition rules
  • Community impact and engagement

6. Terms & Conditions

General Terms

  • All decisions by TrueSelf are final and binding
  • Entries become property of TrueSelf for promotional use
  • Winners will be notified via email within 30 days
  • Prizes must be claimed within 90 days of notification
  • Competition may be modified or cancelled at any time

Privacy & Data

  • Personal information is protected under POPIA
  • Images may be used for marketing purposes
  • Contact details used only for competition purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
